# NEC Learner Notes (Paraphrased)

## Figure 90.3 Structure Summary (Paraphrased)
- NEC has 9 chapters (Annexes are informational only).
- Chapter 1: General.
- Chapter 2: Wiring and Protection.
- Chapter 3: Wiring Methods and Materials.
- Chapter 4: Equipment for General Use.
- Chapter 5: Special Occupancies (supplements/modifies Chapters 1-4 as applicable).
- Chapter 6: Special Equipment (supplements/modifies Chapters 1-4 as applicable).
- Chapter 7: Special Conditions (supplements/modifies Chapters 1-4 as applicable).
- Chapter 8: Communications Systems; not subject to Chapters 1-7 except where specifically referenced.
- Chapter 9: Tables; applicable as referenced.
